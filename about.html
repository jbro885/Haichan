Haichan

How-To/FAQ

Haichan is a new kind of imageboard, where everything can be referenced by transaction IDs on the blockchain.


To get started, you must first make a Money Button account.


If you don't have any BSV, shoot an email to getstarted@haich.us with your Money Button ID or a wallet address.


You can also join our Telegram group or IRC.


To make your first thread, you need an image to post. Go to the "Image Library" and copy one that you like.


Now you're ready to make your first thread. Go to /b/ and copy your image link into the "b:// image link" field.
